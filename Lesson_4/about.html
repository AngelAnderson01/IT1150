All about Fenora

Fenora

Fenora is located on a little island in Nova Scotia. Fenora is about 69 acres big. As of March 2021 there are a little under 500 people living in Fenora. Most people that live here are retired and enjoy the peace and quiet, however most still enjoy working and comute to town most days. We do not see a lot of tourism because of our strict regulations, but when we do allow people to enter our city they are friendly, kind, and always respectful. Even though we have such high standards here, our crime rate is pretty moderate, most of the crimes that take place are violates to our high standards of always being kind and having a smile on your face at all times.

Things to eat!

Most of the time people enjoy lobster, straight out of the ocean. We are also famous for our lobster pies we make when you visit us at the beach - for a fee of course!

Things to do!

During the summer time our citizens enjoy parasailing, scuba diving, whale watching and exploring our amazing forest that has exotic animals and caves!

During our harsh month of winter our citizens enjoy snow boarding and ice fishing!
