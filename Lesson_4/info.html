Info about Fenora

Taxes and Cost of Living

We are such a small island with not many residents, so have a high cost of living. Since none of our residents can leave they all own their homes, with our cheapest property at 1 million dollars. Besides our fruit that we grow, and fish we gather; any imported food runs about 3 times the normal cost.

Our residents pay several different taxes at various rates. Most residents pay 50% of their gross pay in city taxes, but that's okay because all of our residents are weathly and do not need their money from the jobs they work in town anyways!

Learn about our weather and more!

Between May and July, our weather is amazing! 80 degrees fahrenheit and 80% of the time sunny!

Between August and November our average temperature is 65 degrees fahernheit with several rain showers per week.

December is our one month of harsh winter weather with the average temperature around -10 degrees fahrenheit.

Between January and April is our spring time, average temperatures around 55 degrees fahrenheit but we have serious tropical storms around this time of year as well!

During the spring time our pollution is at a medium level because all of our residents have mandatory work in town to do, their commuting causes our pollution levels to rise - but that's okay because they only work from January to April and have the rest of the year off!
