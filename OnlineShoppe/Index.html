About Abstract Paintings

At Abstract Paintings we look for artist that need help selling their paintings.
All proceeds go to the artist that created the painting.
Abstract Paintings is just the middle man for helping artist sell their artwork.

If you would like to submit your abstract painting for sell on our website Please click here!

Disclosure: Abstract Paintings does not own any of the paintings we sell. The artist will ship the painting and shipping normally takes 10-14 business days. Returns or refunds will go through the artist and Abstract Paintings has no contol over returns/refunds. All proceeds go to the artist.
